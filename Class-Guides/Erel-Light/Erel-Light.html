Q. What has changed from the Test Server?

Erel Light seems to have gotten a majority of its bugs fixed. As of right now I currently do not know what bug was left not fixed. It even received some nice benefits as well, like improving the Flash Jump and Up Jump, increasing the invincibility duration of our 4th Job skill Shield of Chulainn from 3 seconds to 10 seconds, buffing the combo shield from 10% to 30%, and more than doubled the damage of the extremely weak Origin, making it feel a lot more impactful.

However, the class received a MASSIVE 30% final damage loss due to losing it in skill passives.  This is an absolutely huge nerf. With the bug fixes, Erel Light is still stronger than it was in the Test Server, but with this FD loss, Erel might maybe only be middle of the pack among all jobs. This is still not confirmed though, as people have not gotten the class far enough for proper confirmation. There also seems to have been a nerf to the M1 (SHINE Spear of Lugh) as well.

Q. How do I unlock my Masteries?

Erda Link starts as a small square that expands outwards the more you unlock skills moving away from the center. In order to get the masteries, you need to unlock the passives that reach outward enough to their point.

M1: Unlock Normal Enemy Damage (or Sol Janus) and DEX.

M2: Unlock DEX and one of the 3 Ignore Defense passives on the right.

Q. How do I cast the 30% Shield?

While holding Spear of Lugh, you can cast Fury of Roan AND Sting of Roan without delay while the Spear of Lugh animation is going on. You don’t press them both at the same time, just press one and then press the other immediately after.
