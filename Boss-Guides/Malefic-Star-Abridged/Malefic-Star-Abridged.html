General

Phases

  • 3 HP phases
  • No untargetable transition between phases (can continue hitting)
  • Illusion  & Real
  • You always spawn in Illusion  at the start
  • Bind persists whether it phases between HP or Illusion  & Real

Orb  Person

  • Designate 1  person in the waiting room to collect Orbs (NPC interact)
  • They are Orb  Person for the entire run (pray for them)
  • Recommend someone with fast reaction speed and/or high mobility
  • Orb  person determines whether you clear or fail
  • They are your only way to reduce gauge ( more later )
  • They choose when to switch between Illusion  & Real
  • Picking up Orbs in Illusion reduces Hunger

Gauges (Read next page for TLDR)

Hunger Gauge  (Predation in KMS translations/government name)

  • Same as erosion/willpower (if it hits 1000, you fail)

Gauge ( Illusion Phase )

  • Yellow Gauge  (Outside)
  • Reduced by Orb  person picking up Orbs
  • Increased by getting hit by attacks
  • Aim for it to be 0 (not lit up)
  • Inside Gauge
  • Slowly increases (fills) over time
  • Boss does “ Purple Attack ” when filled
  • Getting hit by attacks delays filling
  • You want this to fill up as much as possible

Gauge ( Real Phase )

  • Yellow Gauge  
  • 4 parts determine difficulty of Real Phase
  • 1/4 : easy mode
  • 2/4 : getting hit increases Hunger Gauge
  • 3/4 : manta ray spawns
  • 4/4 : all malefic’s attacks deal more damage
  • Inside Gauge
  • Shows how much FD% you are at
  • Does not affect spawn timing of Purple Attack  (it’s fixed in this phase)

The Yellow Gauge  carries over when switching between Illusion and Real , the lower you get it in Illusion, the easier Real   will be.

Real Phase - Towers

In Real Phase  everytime you pick up an Orb , 2 towers (called Radiant pillars in-game) will spawn on either side of the map

Left/black/ purple  tower

Right/white/ yellow  tower

Reduces Yellow Gauge  + Reduces FD%

  • Mechanics get easier, but you do less damage

Increases Yellow Gauge  + Increases FD%

  • Mechanics are harder, but you do more damage

Phasing notes

  • When you are in Illusion Phase
  • Phasing from Illusion  to Real  with 0 Yellow Gauge  will result in 100% FD ; anything higher than 0 Yellow Gauge  will result in 90% FD; 1000 Yellow Gauge  when phasing… please play better… 😭
  • If you let the Illusion Phase  timer get below 1:00, your Hunger will increase slowly until you phase
  • When you are in Real Phase
  • If you phase in with or  reach 1000 Yellow Gauge , your timer is reduced to 0:40 + your Hunger increases every second + Orb  person is unable to switch phases
  • Orb person must pick up an Orb to revert timer back to what it was originally (before it was nerfed to 0:40) + to be able to switch phases again
  • If you cannot reduce it below 1000 Yellow Gauge , she hugs you (FMA) and your hunger increases

Strats

Including but not limited to:

Standard Strat

Ideal execution:

  1. Decrease Yellow Gauge  to 0 while in Illusion  
  2. Enter Real with 0 Yellow Gauge  for 100% FD
  3. Interact with both towers to stay between 115% - 130% FD
  4. Burst with 130% then recover gauge with Left/purple Tower

Variations:

  1. Can be more aggressive with FD% when
  1. Low hunger
  2. Earlier in the fight (p1/p2)
  1. Play safer when
  1. Hunger is high (swap back to Illusion )
  2. Later in the fight

Chill Strat

No risk, but higher spec required

  1. In Illusion , only dodge Orb (Purple) Attacks
  1. Optional: Dodge other Attacks / Map Attacks
  1. When Illusion   timer is close to 1:00 OR  30s until burst, swap to Real  
  2. Swap back to Illusion   after 30s and burst in Illusion

You chill in Illusion phase, you never play Real   phase any longer than you need to.

Sweat Strat

High risk, use for low spec or minclears

  1. Swap to Real  ASAP (Usually after first burst and before 3rd Orb spawns)
  2. When entering Real , FD is 90% due to high Yellow Gauge
  3. Pick up Orb ASAP, increasing FD to 105%
  4. Stay in Real  as long as possible while interacting with Right/yellow Tower
  5. Burst in Real  thereafter with high FD

Yellow Gauge  is usually close to or is maxed out. Increased mechanics to dodge, increased mechanic damage.

Orb (Purple) Attacks ( Illusion  & Real )

Orb Attack 1: Orb Wave

Phase 1+

Spawns in front of the boss, waves of attacks pulse from either side.

Phase 2 & Phase 3

Spawns on the Orb Person’s location

Wait to the side, and dodge into it (to pick up orb)

Orb Attack 2: Orb Dash

Phase 2+

Summon Orb in front of her, 1st dash, Orb activates , 2nd dash, front/back hit

Wait behind the boss, walk in and pick up orb after initial dash, jump away to dodge front/back hit.

Phase 3

Has an additional dash :  1st dash, Summon Orb, 2nd dash, front/back hit, 3rd dash, Orb activates , front/back hit  again
Wait and pick up orb immediately after the 3rd dash (be careful of front/back hit)

Notes:

  • Only the 1st dash hits you, the 2nd and 3rd dashes don’t deal any damage, only the front/back hits
  • The final front/back hit is indicated by the pulsing purple while she is dashing

Orb (Purple) Attacks ( Illusion  & Real ) Cont.

Orb Attack 3: Out Orb In Orb “Lines”

Phase 3

2 sets of lines appear, the first set you dodge

The second set you stand in (purple baldy attacks)

Orbs spawn during  the lines, and activate immediately after  each set

Dodge lines, pick up orb, stand in line, pick up orb

Orb Attack 4: “Keep Moving!”

Phase 3 (Hard Mode Only)

A purple magic circle appears above the boss and shoots you (instanced) 6 times in quick succession

Active Orbs  will spawn randomly (2 in total) with every hit.
Pick up then dodge asap

Illusion  Attacks

Phase 1+

Illusion Attack 1: Behind

Hits in front so  be behind   the boss

Illusion Attack 2: In

Hits the sides so be in side  the boss hitbox

Illusion Attack 3: Black Hole

Spawns on you (instanced) and succs you, dash / teleport / fly (just be a HY) out

Illusion Attack 4: Dash

She holds up a magic circle in front of her, up jump or be behind her

Illusion Attacks Cont.

Phase 2+

Illusion Attack 5: Eyes (so many eyes)

There is an indicator in the top+middle part of the map “showing” which area the eyes will appear (left, middle or right)

Just move away from the area where you see the first few appear

Phase 3

Illusion Attack 6: Line Spears

Magic circles and lines appear on the map indicating where spears will shoot through

The easiest mechanic (imo), stand in a safe spot and let mechanic resolve (they shoot really slow so be patient)

Illusion Map Attacks

Phase 1+

Illusion Map Attack 1: Triangles

A hexagon will appear on the left, middle or right side of the map indicating which side the triangles will spawn from.

Stay close to the hexagon (but not directly on it), and move into where the first one disappears
Phase 2+: There will be 2 sets of triangles moving from the same direction, but the patterns will be slightly different, dodge using the same concept

Illusion Map Attack 2: Imp Fairy Things

Little shitters appear next to you (instanced) and drop a small AOE, don’t be in it or it knocks you back

Illusion Map Attack 3: Lasers

Just dodge them, jump or duck (pretend seren is stabbing you)

Illusion Map Attacks Cont.

Phase 3

Illusion Map Attack 4: Evil Lara Sunlight Thing

A small flower area will appear on the left, middle or right side of the map. If someone walks into it, it triggers and attacks 4 times

Don’t walk into it, if you do, don’t stay there.

Real Attacks

Phase 1+

Real Attack 1: Behind

Hits in front so   be behind   the boss (or duck)

Real Attack 2: Front

Hits the back so be in front  the boss (you cannot  duck this one)

Real Attack 3: Balls

Spawns balls in 3 different fixed patterns, just dodge them.

Real Attacks Cont.

Phase 2+

Real Attack 4: Black Hole

Just don’t be in the danger looking place, no succ involved in this one

Real Attack 5: REAL Black Hole

When you see an actual black hole appear in the black hole from above. Spawns 1 add for each party member that you have to block so it doesn’t hit the boss. They are  not instanced  so you can block them for a party member if you want.

Real Attack 5: REAL Black Hole Cont.

If any of the adds touch the boss, stand directly under the boss in the small purple safe zone.
